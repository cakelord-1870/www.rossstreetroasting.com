DESCRIPTION

The Cloza Estate is a 4th-Generation family farming and micro mill operation run by Kathia Zamora, under the watchful eye of her parents. Cloza is a Women Care Certified (a Certification developed by The International Women Coffee Alliance/Costa Rica) to empower women within the coffee value chain. Her coffee is also Carbon Footprint Friendly, as she participates in the Nama Coffee Programme.

About Kathia's Legacy

  • Flavor notes: Spiced Apple, Caramel, Raisin
  • Roast level: Light-Medium
  • Farm: Coza Estate
  • Farmer: Kathia Zamora
  • Location: Rosario de Naranjo and San Isidro de Alajuela, Costa Rica
  • Farm size: 38 hectares (94 acres)
  • Altitude: 1100-1350 Masl
  • Varieties: Catuai, Villa Sarchi, San Isidro, Victoria
  • Process: Honey

Cloza Estate has been dedicated to growing and processing specialty coffee since 1970. They produce micro-lots in three family plots.

Kathia, a young mother of two boys, is passionate about honoring her legacy while looking towards the future to ensure her kids fall in love with coffee from a young age, just as she did.

Kathia has managed the farm since 2001 and has improved the quality of their coffee by planting new varieties and updating facilities and processing techniques.
